How I help Work with me

A humanist in a noisy AI moment.

I'm Zoe, a product leader who's been coaching enterprise teams through the messy reality of AI through my work. I've sat in the rooms where leaders and knowledge workers are equally confused, and everyone's trying to keep up.

What I've learned: most people don't need more AI tools. They need someone to slow things down, prioritize the business problem worth solving, and match the right tool to the right problem, instead of chasing every AI feature that ships.

Now I coach and build using AI tools, helping people go from passive users to confident builders. The people I work with walk away with more clarity, more consistent AI output, hours back in their week, and sometimes, entirely new ways to grow revenue.

That's why I built ZoeDecodes — to bring this same clarity to non-tech professionals, so you can turn AI noise into useful workflows or solutions.
